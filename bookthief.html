Message

In The Book Thief, Markus Zusak illustrates how literacy and storytelling become a means of survival, emotional resilience, and human connection during times of political unrest and personal trauma. Liesel’s ability to read and write not only empowers her, but also becomes a symbol of resistance against the dehumanizing effects of Nazi ideology.

Evidence

"The words. Why did they have to exist? Without them, there wouldn't be any of this." — Markus Zusak

Liesel uses books to bond with others, gain strength, and resist the darkness of Nazi Germany.
