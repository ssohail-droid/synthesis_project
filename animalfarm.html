Message

In Animal Farm, George Orwell reveals how those in power can manipulate knowledge to control society. The pigs deliberately limit the other animals’ access to education and truth, using rewritten commandments and propaganda to solidify their dominance, exposing the danger of intellectual inequality in any community.

Evidence

"All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others." — George Orwell

The pigs alter language and suppress education to stay in control, showing the danger of knowledge in the wrong hands.
